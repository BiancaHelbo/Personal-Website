I'm a 21 year old Danish girl from a suburbian town in Denmark. I grew up in a nice town close to the beach, with my mom, my dad and my younger brother.
We used to travel a lot and we've been to many different locations; Aruba, The Dominican Republic, France, Italy, and it was always great to experience other cultures and seeing so many different things.
One of my favorite locations were Aruba where we went snorkeling and experienced fishes of so many different colours and shapes.
Three years ago we decided to get a dog, a Golden Retriever that we named Marley. We stopped traveling, but enjoyed the time with our lovely dog.

Since I was very young, I’ve had a great passion for computer games and computers in general and when I got older, I developed yet another passion for the internet and making small webpages for fun. The internet is a big part of my daily life and I love looking at beautifully, cleverly created websites.
This passion is what drives me towards becoming a multimedia designer who designs useful and beautiful webpages etc. for other people to look at and enjoy during their daily lives.

Another big part of my life is music. I started playing the cello when I was about 7 years old, but stopped when I didn't feel like it was the right intrument for me.
I then started singing in a church choir, where I ended up staying for 8 years.
In the meantime I started playing the piano, which I still do occasionally, but I mostly taught myself.

Now I live in Copenhagen with my boyfriend and my cat. I study at KEA and I look forward to becoming a great Multimedia Designer in the future.
