A hobby of mine is video games. I've played games since I was little where it started as childrens games. Then I moved on to RPGs, Role Playing Games, such as The Elder Scrolls series, and I ended up at MMORPGs, Massively Multiplayer Online Role Playing Games, with World of Warcraft as the one I'm still playing on my 8th year.
I like playing WoW because of the community and the experience of playing with other people from all over Europe. The guild I'm in, I've been in for 5 years and I've met so many wonderful people. Three nights a week we meet up to raid. The thrill of getting a boss down that you've worked on for several weeks, is just so nice.
Especially when it's with your guildies.

I enjoy exploring other video games aswell. I love immersing myself in the stories that other people have come up with, and feeling when a developer has put a lot of effort into a video game.
