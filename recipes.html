FLUFFY PASSION FRUIT SOUFFLÉ IN A MUG

A really nice and simple dessert from one of my favorite Youtube channels, SORTED food.

Ingredients:

  • 3 passion fruits
  • A knob of butter, softened
  • 2 eggs (2 whites, but just 1 yolk)
  • 75g caster sugar, plus extra to line the mugs

Recipe:

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C
  2. Cut the passion fruit in half and remove the pulp with a tea spoon
  3. Strain the pulp through a sieve, massaging the pips with the back of a spoon
  4. Grease the inside of two mugs with the butter and sprinkle with a little sugar
  5. Beat the sugar and egg yolk with the passion fruit juice until pale and creamy
  6. Fold the egg whites into the passion mixture in two batches
  7. Spoon the passion fruit seeds into the base of the mugs
  8. Divide between two mugs and bake for 8 minutes until puffed up and golden.
